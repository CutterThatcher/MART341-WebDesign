How to Write a Check

What You Will Need

  • A blank check
  • A pen (Not a pencil)

Step 1: Date the Check

Step 2: Pay to the Order of

Here, put the name of who you are paying.

Step 3: Numeric Amount

Write the amount you are paying in the box on the right using numbers.

Step 4: Written Amount

Write out the amount you are paying using words on the line below "Pay to the Order of."

Step 5: For/Memo

Write a note here as to why you are paying.

Step 6: Signature

Sign the check so it shows that you are giving the other person permission to take your money.
