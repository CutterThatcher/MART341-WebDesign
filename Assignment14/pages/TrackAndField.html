The University of Montana Track and Field Team has earned a distinguished reputation for its outstanding achievements and the remarkable performances of its athletes. Over the years, the team has consistently excelled in various track and field disciplines, showcasing the depth of talent nurtured within the program. Notable achievements include numerous conference titles and commendable showings in NCAA competitions. The team's success is a testament to the dedication of both coaches and athletes who work tirelessly to reach the highest levels of performance.

Several athletes have left an indelible mark on the University of Montana Track and Field legacy. Among them, names like Lindsey Hall and Alex Mustard shine brightly. Lindsey Hall, a former middle-distance runner, displayed exceptional prowess on the track, earning All-American honors and securing multiple Big Sky Conference titles during her time with the team. Alex Mustard, a standout pole vaulter, soared to great heights, setting school records and contributing significantly to the team's success. These athletes, along with many others, have not only brought acclaim to the University of Montana Track and Field program but have also become inspirational figures for aspiring athletes within and beyond the university community.
