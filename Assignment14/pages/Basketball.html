The University of Montana Basketball Team, known as the Grizzlies, has been a formidable presence in collegiate basketball, showcasing both skill and determination on the court. Notable achievements for the program include multiple conference championships and appearances in the NCAA Tournament, underscoring the team's consistent competitiveness at the highest levels. The Grizzlies' success is a testament to the coaching staff's strategic leadership and the dedication of the athletes who have proudly worn the team colors. The Dahlberg Arena, home to the Grizzlies, becomes a lively battleground during home games, with fans rallying behind the team in a spirited display of support.



Throughout its history, the University of Montana Basketball Team has produced remarkable athletes who have left an enduring impact. Notable figures include Larry Krystkowiak, a former Grizzlies player who went on to have a successful NBA career and later became a respected coach. The team has also seen the talents of players like Will Cherry and Martin Breunig, both of whom contributed significantly to the Grizzlies' success during their respective tenures. These athletes, along with others, have contributed to the rich legacy of the University of Montana Basketball program, leaving a lasting imprint on the history of collegiate basketball in Montana.
