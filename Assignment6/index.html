Tater Tot Taco Casserole!


Ingredients

  • 1 lb of lean ground beef
  • 1 diced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic minced
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 1 cup of enchilada sauce
  • 1.5 cups of frozen corn
  • 1.5 cups of diced bell peppers
  • 1 12-oz can of black beans
  • 3 cups shredded cheese
  • 16-oz bag of frozen tater tots
  • 1/3 cup of salsa

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Brown ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium high until no pink remains. Drain any fat
  3. Stir in taco seasoning, 1/2 cup of water, 2/3 cup of enchilada sauce, corn, bell peppers, and beans. Cook until thickened About 5 minutes
  4. Pour beef mixture into a 9x13 pan. Sprinkle with 1.5 cups of cheese and arrange tater tots on top
  5. Bake for 30 minutes. Combine salsa and remaining 1/3 cup of enchilada sauce and spread over tater tots and top with remaining cheese.
  6. Bake an additional 10 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly
  7. Serve with desired toppings
Don't forget to listen to some music while you cook!
